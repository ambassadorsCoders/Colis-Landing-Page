SPRING FASHION SHOW 2025

THE GREEN IN THE WILD







The Spring Fashion Runway 2025 embraced the theme "The Green in the Wild," blending nature’s vibrant hues with untamed elegance. Designers drew inspiration from lush forests and sprawling meadows, showcasing a range of green shades from deep emeralds to soft moss tones. Flowing fabrics mimicked nature’s fluidity, while earthy textures and botanical prints celebrated the raw beauty of the wild. This season’s collection highlighted sustainability, merging eco-conscious materials with cutting-edge fashion, creating a balance between creativity and the green expanses of our planet.
